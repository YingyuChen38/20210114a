Our Kindergarden Features

At Spring's Kids, success is not only excelling academically; success is achieving your full potential; success is discovering a love for learning; success is engaging creatively; success is being a kind citizen; success is being yourself; success is a group accomplishment.

Spring's Kids Kindergarten is a special environment and a key component in a child's experience. A qualified, motivated teaching team make the facility a joyful and exciting place to be, understanding that their role is to provide a supportive partnership for Spring's Kids School's parents seeking the very best education for their children. Kindergarten at Spring's Kids School does not miss the opportunity for their kids to learn while they are still young and at their most adept learning phase. The kids are immersed in a captivating environment where they learn Chinese, English and creative skills such as music, dance and arts and crafts. In addition Spring's Kids School takes pride in teaching the importance of discipline and good behaviour so they progress as mature students, likely to be successful during and after their studies.
